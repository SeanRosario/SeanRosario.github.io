Research & Projects

please scroll down

Kickstarter Success Prediction: Using open Kickstarter data with scraped data to build a predictive model which would determine probability of success given various project attributes.
​
​Taxi vs. weather: Analyzing how the NYC taxi pick up rate and fare rates are affected by changes in weather like rain, snow, etc, 

Yelp Business Category Prediction: Using ﻿Yelp's academic data set﻿, we applied machine learning algorithms such as Naive Bayes, k-NN, and linear SVM, to predict the category of a new Yelp business


Campus Crime Analysis:  Developed an interactive tool to display visualizations and statistical summaries of university (on-campus) crimes, based on a user’s choice of input


​Headed a team to develop an online student information portal
under guidance of the institute Director – Prof. Dr. RN Saha


Written a paper: “Obstacle detection and path navigation by autonomous mobile robots”, 
under supervision of Professor Dr. Anand Kumar.
This paper was awarded best student paper at BITS Pilani, Dubai and shortlisted for IEEE international student paper competition. 


Project: Development of “Smartphone Application for Android OS implementing Near Field Communication (NFC) based security” under guidance of Prof. Dr. Jagadish Nayak at BITS Pilani


Research on the use of Applied Statistics & Data Analysis for better business decisions, using regression & correlation in IBM SPSS, under guidance of Prof. Dr. Maneesha Bhagchandani, BITS Pilani


Completed a group project at Harvard University on the market share and potential of Caffe Bene in the USA, personally commended by Mr. John Barry, U.S. Director of Franchising for Caffe Bene


Submitted a group paper at Harvard University on “iDiffusion - extending the concept of Diffusion of innovation”. The paper was recommended for publication by Prof. Dr. Cherian Thachenkary


Co-founded an e-commerce venture - “Partycation”, with students from Harvard University. Venture highly commended by Prof. Dr. Dennis Galletta
